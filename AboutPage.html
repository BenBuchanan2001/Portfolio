Let's talk about me.

I've always had a passion for tech and programming since my teenage years. From wanting to understand how things are made to making a new app and becoming the next Mark Zuckerberg or a game developer for a renowned AAA studio, I was eager to break into the field. However, I lacked guidance and patience, so eventually after trial and error I dropped the dream and focused on my Xbox and hanging out with friends like any teen would do.

However, fast forward six years, I found myself working at a tech recruitment agency, connecting with inspiring professionals in the industry. Their stories reignited my desire to become a software developer. So, I bid farewell to recruitment, joined a boot camp, and I've never looked back since.
